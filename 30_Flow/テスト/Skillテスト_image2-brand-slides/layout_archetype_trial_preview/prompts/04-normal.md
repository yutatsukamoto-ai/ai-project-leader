Layout archetype: evidence_board.

Content:
- Navigation title: 成功条件
- Message headline: PoC前に判断基準を固定する
- Supporting message: 成功条件、レビュー観点、リスクを先に置くほど、PoCの評価がぶれにくい。

- Emphasis reason: emphasize the criteria themselves, not the item count.
- Supporting facts:
  - 成功条件
  - レビュー観点
  - リスク

Layout:
- One clear headline, a short supporting message, and four supporting evidence rows.
- Avoid dense dashboard.
- Do not make the number of items visually dominant.
- No bottom message band.

Negative constraints:
- No generic metrics dashboard.
- No tiny charts everywhere.
- No oversized item count.
- No page numbers.
