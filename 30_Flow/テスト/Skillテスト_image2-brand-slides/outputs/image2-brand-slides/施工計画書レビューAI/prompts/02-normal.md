# 02-normal

Create a single 16:9 slide image.
Do not create a contact sheet.
Do not include multiple slides in one image.
The slide body/content must be Image2-generated.
Do not use SVG, code-rendered layout, wireframe, programmatic vector graphics, HTML canvas, Mermaid, or screenshots for the body content.
Use the provided brand system faithfully.
Make Japanese text as accurate and readable as possible.
Do not imitate unrelated templates.
Use a simple consulting-style design.
Do not show page numbers anywhere on the slide.
Do not include sequence numbers in visible slide titles.
Do not create or invent a logo.

Brand system:
- No-logo deck.
- Background: warm off-white.
- Primary: deep green.
- Accent: restrained amber.
- Text: dark gray.
- Thin clean rules, generous margins, low decoration.
- Noto Sans JP style, thin Japanese sans-serif typography.

Normal-slide navigation:
- Small slide title at the exact same top-left position across normal slides.
- Short thin underline below the title.
- In no-logo mode, do not create or invent a logo. Leave the top-right area empty.
- The title should be a quiet navigation element, not the main content.
- Do not show page numbers.
- Do not prefix the title with numbers such as "1.", "01", or "第1章".

Slide title:
本日の流れ

Key message:
背景、課題、AIの役割、PoC、成功条件の順に合意形成する。

Slide content:
Create five calm horizontal or vertical flow blocks:
- 背景
- 現状課題
- AIの役割
- PoCの進め方
- 成功条件

Add a small bottom callout:
まずは「AIが何をしないか」を明確にしてからPoC範囲を決める。

Negative constraints:
- No visible page number.
- No numbered title.
- No logo.
- No overly colorful blocks.
- No busy dashboard.
- No robot or futuristic AI illustration.
