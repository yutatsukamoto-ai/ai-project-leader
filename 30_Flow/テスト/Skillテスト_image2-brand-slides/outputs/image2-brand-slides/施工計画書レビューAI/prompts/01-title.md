# 01-title

Create a single 16:9 slide image.
Do not create a contact sheet.
Do not include multiple slides in one image.
The slide body/content must be Image2-generated.
Do not use SVG, code-rendered layout, wireframe, programmatic vector graphics, HTML canvas, Mermaid, or screenshots for the body content.
Do not imitate unrelated templates.
Do not show page numbers anywhere on the slide.
Do not include sequence numbers in visible slide titles.
Do not create or invent a logo.

Brand system:
- No-logo deck.
- Warm off-white background.
- Deep green primary color, restrained amber accent.
- Simple consulting-style presentation design.
- Generous whitespace, low decoration, thin rules, clean grids.
- Noto Sans JP style: thin, clean Japanese sans-serif typography; Regular for headings, Light for body text, Medium only for emphasis.
- Avoid heavy bold typography, futuristic AI decoration, robots, glowing gradients, or construction-site stock-photo backgrounds.

Title slide:
- Output only this one title slide as a standalone image.
- The entire title slide, including content area, typography, background, and visual accents, must be Image2-generated.
- In no-logo mode, do not create or invent a logo.
- Keep the design calm, practical, and suitable for a construction company internal briefing.
